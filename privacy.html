Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

1. Our Privacy Commitment

Springroll Team is built on a foundation of data sovereignty. Your documents, files, and AI interactions stay on your local device. We designed our system to minimize data collection while providing a powerful AI workstation experience.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Name and profile picture (from Google)
  • Subscription status and billing history

Usage Analytics (Optional)

  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Error reports and crash logs
  • Application performance metrics

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your documents, files, or folder contents
  • AI prompts or generated outputs
  • Keyboard input or screen recordings
  • Browsing history or personal files

3. Local Processing

Springroll Team runs AI models locally on your GPU. Your documents are processed entirely on your device. We cannot see, access, or retrieve your local data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and verify subscription status
  • Process payments through Stripe (we don't store card details)
  • Send important service updates and security notices
  • Improve our products based on aggregated, anonymized usage data

5. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google OAuth Authentication Email, name, profile image
Stripe Payment processing Email, payment details
Supabase Account database Email, subscription status

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, deleted upon request
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; identifiable data deleted after 90 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@springroll.ai.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3), secure authentication, and regular security audits. Your local data is protected by your device's security.

9. Children's Privacy

Springroll Team is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International Users

Our servers are located in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to data processing in the U.S. For EU users, we comply with GDPR requirements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

12. Contact Us

Privacy questions? Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@springroll.ai.
