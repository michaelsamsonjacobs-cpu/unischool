# LegalWork Pro Workflow

Securely manage your Intellectual Property (IP) locally before filing.

## Phase 1: Disclosure Intake
*   Describe your invention or brand in plain English.
*   Attach any technical diagrams or sketches.

## Phase 2: Prior Art Search
*   Agent scans USPTO and Google Patents via the Gemini Bridge.
*   Identify potential conflicts or overlapping IP.

## Phase 3: Drafting
*   **Patents**: Generate claims sets and detailed descriptions.
*   **Trademarks**: Generate class descriptions and use statements.

## Phase 4: Filing Prep
*   Export as .docx formatted for the patent office.
*   Review by the agent for technical consistency.
